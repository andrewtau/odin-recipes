Special Fries Recipe

Description

This recipe is for a Special French Fry Dish.

Ingredients

  • Frozen fry cut of your choice (We'll be using waffle)
  • Bacon
  • Shredded Cheese of your choice (We'll be using Colby Jack)
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Chili Powder(Optional)

Steps

  1. First we'll start off by creating the seasoning for the fries to be tossed in once they've finished cooking. In a mixing bowl, combine all the seasonings together and mix around until thoroughly combined. There's your simple seasoning!

  2. Now cook your french fries as per instructions.

  3. While the fries are cooking, we can prepare the bacon. Slice the bacon into small bite size pieces and cook on a pan. Once cooked, drain the Oil and place on the side until fries are finished.

  4. Once the Fries have finished, immediately combine them with your seasoning either in a seperate bowl or the same bowl. Toss until combined.

  5. Now place your fries in a bowl and to that bowl you're going to add your shredded cheese of choice on top. Place into the microwave for 30s to melt the cheese.

  6. Once the cheese is melted, you can place your bacon bits as well as sour cream on top.

  7. That's all! Serve with your choice of condiments. Chef's tips: These taste extremely good with nacho cheese. Trust me, don't knock it until you've tried it.
